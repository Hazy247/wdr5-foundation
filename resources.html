Evaluating information

Choose sources carefully

Rare-condition information can change quickly and quality varies. Prefer resources that identify their authors, cite evidence, show a review date and separate established knowledge from emerging ideas.

  • Check when the resource was last reviewed.
  • Discuss medical claims with a qualified clinician.
  • Be cautious of products or treatments promising guaranteed results.
  • Protect personal and genetic information when joining online services.
